About

"Mogilev in 1943 | Then and Now" is a web mapping application designed to:

  • Provide a visual comparison between historical aerial photography from 1943 and current web maps of Mogilev, allowing users to explore urban landscape changes since World War II.
  • Serve as an educational tool for students and researchers interested in urban development and historical geography.
